Description

Grand Cru: The Winemaker is a Bordeaux winemaking simulation where you inherit a struggling château, cultivate vineyards across 6 real communes, craft wines using authentic techniques, and negotiate with 25 unique NPC buyers to build your reputation.

  • 6 Bordeaux communes: Pauillac, Saint-Julien, Margaux, Saint-Émilion, Pomerol, Haut-Médoc
  • 6 grape varieties with authentic characteristics
  • 4-pillar wine scoring: Structure, Typicity, Finish, Complexity
  • 25 NPC buyers across 6 types (collectors, courtiers, chefs, importers, and more)
  • 7 rival AI wineries competing in the same market
  • 50+ story and random events
  • Bordeaux assemblage (blending) system
  • Dual scoring: critic taste vs. public taste
  • 4-season cycle with weather affecting each vintage
  • Father's wine mystery storyline

Key Features

Authentic Bordeaux Winemaking

Manage vineyards across 6 real communes of Bordeaux. Each commune has distinct terroir affecting grape quality, and weather changes every season to create unique vintages.

Deep Crafting System

Control every step from grape to glass: fermentation sliders, oak aging, blending ratios. Master the Bordeaux assemblage tradition to create your signature cuvee.

25 Unique NPC Buyers

Negotiate with collectors, courtiers, chefs, importers, hypermarkets, and chain outlets. Each buyer has distinct preferences, budgets, and personality-driven haggling styles.

4-Pillar Scoring System

Wines are evaluated on Structure, Typicity, Finish, and Complexity. Critics and the public weigh these differently, creating strategic tension in how you craft and price your wines.

Living Economy

7 rival AI wineries compete for the same buyers and awards. Market share shifts dynamically as reputations rise and fall. Pricing is a constant strategic decision.

Story-Driven Events

50+ events including a multi-chapter story campaign about your father's legendary wine. Visual novel sequences, rival confrontations, and branching choices shape your journey.
